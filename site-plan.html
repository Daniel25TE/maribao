Maribao Hotel of General Villamil (Site Name)

This name represents a hotel in the city of General Villamil (Ecuador), Maribao is the name of the village where the hotel is located and it means "FUN". Also Maribao is known for its beaches and kind people, the name is a slang that African people brought to that part of the coast of Ecuador and the owner of the hotel has been using it to don't forget their culture and where they come from.

Optional domain availability: maribaos.com

For the Maribao Hotel website (Site Purpose)

The purpose of the site is to show a general view of the hotel along with different activities to do in the city of General Villamil, it will have a home page with general information about the hotel and its surroundings such as pictures of the place, prices, services, accomodations for special events and other pages will have specific information about each room and a way to reserve them along with a form.
