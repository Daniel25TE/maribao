Non Profit Membership

Price: Free

The Non-Profit Membership provides free access to community events, networking opportunities, training resources focused on nonprofit growth, and inclusion in a nonprofit directory for better visibility. Members also receive discounts on select workshops and consultations and have the opportunity to apply for funding assistance programs to help support their mission.

Bronze Membership

Price: $50

With the Bronze Membership, members enjoy all the benefits of the Non-Profit tier plus additional perks like a spotlight feature on the homepage for greater visibility, access to exclusive members-only webinars and Q&A sessions, priority access to discounted event tickets, and basic advertising opportunities through newsletters and social media. They also become eligible for special grants and funding partnerships, providing additional financial support.

Silver Membership

Price: $100

The Silver Membership takes engagement a step further by building on the Bronze benefits, offering an extended spotlight on the homepage, premium training sessions on industry trends and business development, and one-on-one consultations with experts to refine strategies. Members receive enhanced advertising in newsletters, social media, and website promotions, VIP access to events with discounted tickets and priority seating, and opportunities for deeper connections within the community.

Gold Membership

Price: $200

At the highest level, the Gold Membership includes all the benefits of Silver but adds exclusive sponsorship opportunities for events, personalized business promotion across multiple platforms, and unlimited consultations with industry experts for tailored guidance. Gold members receive high-impact advertising placements, including homepage banners, first access to funding programs and partnership initiatives, and invitations to exclusive networking dinners with top professionals, ensuring maximum visibility and influence in the industry.
